Using KrowdFire

There are several ways to participate as a Member of the KROWDFIRE Website once you have joined as a Member.

Become a Supporter and donate to a Campaign you feel is interesting or worthy. In doing so, you will be supporting someone in a manner similar to other donation-driven Crowdfunding Websites. A donation can be for $25, $50 or $75.

Start your fundraiser

Become a Fundraiser with the intention to create your own Campaign. After being invited to “Join Us”, you begin this process by donating $75 – one time only – as directed by the person who invited you to KROWDFIRE or if you are a Walk-In Visitor to the website, you can begin by requesting an invitation.

Members can and are encouraged to donate to other campaigns

As a Member, you are always eligible to donate to a current Campaign at any time and help support that Campaign Creator.

And at any time, you can decide to become a Fundraiser and begin that fundraising process for yourself. Simply support the Campaign of the person who invited you to KROWDFIRE – or request an “invitation” as previously described.
