Make Good Decisions When Choosing People You Invite To Join You

When thinking about who you might want to invite - and who would be a really good participant - you need to face a candid truth. Not everyone is a good prospect for a KROWDFIRE Crowdfunding Campaign – and some will be much better than others. Perhaps this is a good place to tactfully trot out the old, but accurate expression – “Garbage in – garbage out”. Pick wisely and your Campaign will prosper. Do so randomly or foolishly - and you will spin your wheels and be disappointed with your results.

Become a “Nectar Detector”

A hummingbird must visit 1,800 flowers a day in order to find enough nectar to survive. Some flowers have nectar and some don’t. The hummingbird can’t afford to waste time with nectar-less flowers. Otherwise, she doesn’t get enough nectar to survive - and SPLAT! She crashes and burns. Some people will “get” KROWDFIRE and its potential to help them – and some won’t. Don’t waste too much time with those who don’t. Be polite, kind and courteous – thank them for their time – and then move on to better flowers.
